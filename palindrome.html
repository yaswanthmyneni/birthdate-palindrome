Enter your birth-date and we will tell you if your birth-date is a palindrome

This app checks your birth-date in 6 formats dd-mm-yyyy, mm-dd-yyyy, yyyy-mm-dd, dd-mm-yy, mm-dd-yy, yy-mm-dd

e.g. if your birth-date is 01 Aug 1995, then app will check for 01-08-1995, 08-01-1995, 1995-08-01, 01-08-95, 08-01-95, 95-08-01
